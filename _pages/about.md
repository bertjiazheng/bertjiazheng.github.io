I am currently a research engineer at [KooLab](https://www.kujiale.com/gh/koolab/p/koolab#/home) in [Kujiale.com](http://kujiale.com), working with [Dr. Rui Tang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rui-tang-50973488/) and [Prof. Zihan Zhou](https://faculty.ist.psu.edu/zzhou/). Previously, I obtained my master's degree from <a href="http://www.shanghaitech.edu.cn/" target="_blank">ShanghaiTech University</a> working with <a href="https://sist.shanghaitech.edu.cn/sist_en/2020/0814/c7582a54772/page.htm" target="_blank">Prof. Shenghua Gao</a>, and obtained my bachelor's degree from <a href="http://www.nwpu.edu.cn" target="_blank">Northwestern Polytechnical University</a>.

I am interested in computer vision and machine learning, particularly in the areas of 3D Vision. My current research focuses on recovering 3D geometry from sensor data by utilizing shape regularities.
